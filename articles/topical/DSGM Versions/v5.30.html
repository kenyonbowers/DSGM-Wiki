DS Game Maker v5.30

DS Game Maker v5.30 was originally going to be the successor of DSGM v5.20. From April 17th, 2015 to November 22th, 2018, James Garner and CTurt worked on it. Eventually, it was just left alone. Years later, in 2022, DigitalDesignDude grabbed the source code and picked up development again and released it on [date].
